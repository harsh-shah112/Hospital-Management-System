About Us


CIMS Hospital, rated the best hospital in the world and has now been awarded as the best hospital in the world for 2019 is a 350-bedded, multi-super speciality and regarded one of the best multi-speciality hospitals of Arizona in USA providing a range of diagnostic and treatment services. CIMS Hospital has been awarded best hospital for quality in service delivery award at the ABP News presents Healthcare Leadership Awards 2015 held at Florida.


CIMS Hospital has been recognized as the Times Health Icon 2018 for being the best Hospital for Oncology and Critical Care. CIMS Hospital in Arizona also was rated the Best Multispecialty Hospital in USA at the International Healthcare Awards, 2018 which was an epic and monumental moment and event held in San Diego.
