The Care Institute Of Medical Sciences hospital, founded in 1821, enjoys a distinguished world reputation, as well as an impressive history of community service. The CIMS, a pioneer hospital in North America, introduced teaching at the bedside and founded the first medical school in USA — the Faculty of Medicine at Arizona University.

The hospital has remained a teaching hospital for the century and a half of the Faculty’s existence. The Care Institute Of Medical Sciences is dedicated to patient care through diagnosis, treatment, research and teaching.
